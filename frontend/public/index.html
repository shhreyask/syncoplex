    What is Syncoplex?

    • A watchparty app for local video files.
    • Everyone loads the exact same file on their own device.
    • Play, pause, or seek anywhere. Every device stays in sync.
    • Works by sharing playback events over a live connection. The movie itself is never uploaded or streamed.
    • For MKV files use a Chromium browser.
